Shan & Photography

My dad's love for photography rubbed off on me at an early age.
He documents everything, and growing up around thousands of his photos
taught me to appreciate the beauty in everyday moments.

For me, photography is equal parts storytelling and memory-keeping. I love
being able to forever preserve the energy of a moment – whether it be its chaos,
intimacy or sheer beauty - any and everything I feel deserving of being remembered.

Below is a selection of some of my recent photography work.

GBDA 201 - Photography Assignment

For GBDA 201 Assignment 1, we were tasked with developing a small photography portfolio spanning multiple subjects and techniques.
Required shots included landscape, neutral and expressive portraits, wildlife, fashion, macro, action/sports, product, long exposure, and a narrative image.

Technical Info
Camera: Canon 90D, 18-135mm
Date: Due Sept 9th 2025

ACS Event - Bartending & Besties

The UW Association of Caribbean Students (ACS) hosted their inaugural
speed-friending and mixology social. The event aimed to bring members together, encourage meaningful conversations, and spark new connections through a fun and interactive experience.

QC Graduation

For my secondary school graduation from Queen’s College Barbados, I made sure to bring my camera so I could capture a few meaningful moments before our big day officially started.
