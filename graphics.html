Shan & Graphics Design

My love for graphic design began with creating thumbnails for my
YouTube channel. I continued to develop it by managing the graphics
for several social media pages at my secondary school, which taught me
how to design posters, informational graphics, and more.

ACS Event Posters

I’m the Graphics & Media Lead for the uWaterloo Association of Caribbean
Students (ACS), a role that allows me to be creative while advocating for my
culture. I also have the opportunity to work closely with our vice-president,
an upper-year GBDA student who mentors me throughout the design process.

Physiocare Gift Cards

My mom owns Physiocare Barbados, a physiotherapy clinic that offers
gift cards for treatments and massages.


I redesigned the gift card from Shopify’s default layout into a custom,
branded design that better represents her clinic’s identity.
