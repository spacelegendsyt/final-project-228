About me

Hey there! My name is Shannon. I’m from Barbados but I moved to Canada to study Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) at the University of Waterloo.

I grew up with a father who loved taking photos, and his passion for documenting life quickly became my own. That interest expanded into YouTube and videography, where I spent my childhood filming with friends and teaching myself how to edit, brand, and tell stories.

Over time, photography, videography, design, and marketing all merged into one creative path, which led me to GBDA. I’ve already had the opportunity to explore the professional side of this work at PwC, where I created promotional content and learned how my skills can fit into real marketing environments.

Wherever I end up working in the future, I hope to keep capturing everyday moments and shaping them into stories worth remembering.
